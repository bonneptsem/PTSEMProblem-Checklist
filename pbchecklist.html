Problem Statement Checklist


Checklist:

Is the issue specific and well-defined?
Is it clear and to the point? Do you avoid unnecessary jargon or overly complex language?
Does the statement focus on one primary problem instead of multiple issues?
Do you describe the setting or circumstances where the problem occurs?
Do you mention relevant statistics or data to illustrate the scope of the issue?
Have you included a brief, human-centered story or example that puts a face to the problem?
Have you explained the potential consequences of not addressing the problem?
Does the statement convey why this issue requires immediate attention?

Does the problem statement lead naturally to the solution and ask?
Would someone unfamiliar with the issue immediately understand its significance?
Does it evoke a sense of concern or urgency in the listener?
